Educational Outreach Activity, Virginia Tech, Blacksburg, VA, USA

Served as a Graduate Student Facilitator in PDAL 2026, a teacher immersion program at Virginia Tech. Assisted <a href="https://www.bse.vt.edu/about/people/faculty/justin-barone.html" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Prof. Justin Barone</a> in conducting the workshop "Chemistry and Physics of Gummy Bears," engaging secondary school teachers in hands-on food science education and demonstrating how ingredients and processing influence gummy texture and flavor.
