Educational Outreach Activity, Virginia Tech, Blacksburg, VA, USA

Served as a **Graduate Student Facilitator** in the **Professional Development for Agricultural Literacy (PDAL) 2026**, a teacher immersion program at Virginia Tech. **Facilitated the workshop "Chemistry and Physics of Gummy Bears"** with **Prof. Justin Barone**, engaging secondary school teachers in hands-on food science education and demonstrating how ingredients and processing influence gummy texture and flavor.
